Manual Process 

Sales Colleagues/Business Development Officers

- Have certain clients/businesses on their list
- Go to their website and keep checking for open positions
- Once they see an opening,
    - They go through the details of the job
    - Find possible matches in the current team
    - Look for similar projects that the company has worked on
    - Do some research about the company
- Write a personalized Email

Problem  : 
- They have to write hundreds of possible leads

Practical Use 

Consulting Companies
- Getting clients is crucial for any business
- Check for open positions
- Cold emails to potential clients
    - Personalized
    - Relevant to the Job Post
    - Figure out what information is need to be shared
    - Share relevant portfolios
